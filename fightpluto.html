The crew decides to fight the group of space pirates.The pirates are heavily armed but the crew is prepared.

Photon beams and beam katanas are drawn as both of the crews size eachother up. The first shot is fired and a battle royale insues. Ducking, dodging, clashing, and explosions are all we see as our crew takes the upper hand. Winning the battle, the crew decides to let the defeated pirates go as they continue exploring the planet.
